Frequently Asked Questions

Is my identity visible to my college?

No. Complaints are anonymous by design. We use cryptographic hashing to ensure your identity remains confidential. No login or personal information is required for anonymous reporting.

What if my complaint is ignored?

Our system has automatic escalation protocols. If a complaint isn't addressed within 72 hours, it's escalated to higher authorities and external oversight bodies.

Can this system be misused?

We use AI to detect spam, fake reports, and abuse patterns. Repeated false reporting leads to temporary blocking of device signatures while maintaining anonymity.

Is the SOS feature always active?

Yes. SOS works 24/7 with real-time location tracking and immediate routing to campus security and local emergency services when activated.

How do institutes benefit?

Institutes get a secure dashboard to manage complaints, analytics on campus safety, and the ability to demonstrate proactive care to students and parents.
